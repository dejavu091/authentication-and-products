About Seatle-lgbtq

We are a community-driven nonprofit organization based in Seattle dedicated to supporting LGBTQ+ individuals, especially those experiencing homelessness, housing insecurity, or social exclusion. Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive, and empowering environment where every person is treated with dignity, respect, and compassion. Founded on the belief that no one should be left behind, we provide direct support services including emergency shelter assistance, food programs, mental health referrals, clothing drives, and transitional housing support. Beyond immediate relief, we are committed to long-term empowerment through advocacy, skills development programs, and community reintegration initiatives. We recognize that many LGBTQ+ individuals face unique challenges such as discrimination, family rejection, and limited access to essential resources. Our organization stands as a bridge of hope, ensuring that help is always within reach and that every individual has the opportunity to rebuild their life in safety and stability. Through partnerships, volunteer efforts, and community engagement, we continue to expand our reach and impact. We envision a future where every LGBTQ+ person in Seattle can live freely, proudly, and securely, without fear of homelessness or marginalization.
